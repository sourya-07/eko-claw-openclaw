# Eko Partner Operations & Relationship Policies

**Document ID:** EKO-OPS-POL-003  
**Version:** 5.0  
**Owner:** Channel Operations & Partner Success Team  
**Last Updated:** June 2026

## 1. Partner Onboarding Requirements & Timelines
Any micro-entrepreneur, distributor, or API partner joining Eko must submit:
1. e-KYC (Aadhaar & PAN)
2. Bank account proof (cancelled check or bank statement)
3. Shop commercial registration proof (e.g., Gumasta license, GSTIN, or local municipal trade certificate)
- **SLA:** Automated onboarding takes 10 minutes. If manual review is triggered due to verification mismatch, the SLA is **24 working hours**.

## 2. Commission Structure & Payout Schedules
Eko works on a transaction fee/commission sharing model.
- **DMT (Domestic Money Transfer):** The partner receives a percentage-based commission of up to 0.50% of the transaction volume.
- **AePS (Aadhaar Enabled Payment System):** Flat commission structure based on transaction slab:
  - ₹100 - ₹1,000: ₹1 commission
  - ₹1,001 - ₹3,000: ₹5 commission
  - ₹3,001 - ₹10,000: ₹10 commission
- **Payout Schedule:** Real-time settlement to the merchant's Eko trade wallet. Merchant can cash out/settle to their primary bank account at any time (instant IMPS charge of ₹5 applies).

## 3. Partner Tier System
Partners are categorized into tiers based on monthly Gross Transaction Volume (GTV):
- **Bronze Tier:** GTV < ₹1,00,000 per month.
  - *Benefits:* Standard commission rates, email/app support.
- **Silver Tier:** GTV between ₹1,00,000 and ₹5,00,000 per month.
  - *Benefits:* +5% extra commission payout on select services, priority ticket queuing.
- **Gold Tier:** GTV > ₹5,00,000 per month.
  - *Benefits:* Customized commissions, dedicated relationship manager, zero settlement fees for the first 3 cash-outs daily.

## 4. Performance Metrics & Review Process
Every quarter, partners are evaluated on:
- **Transaction Success Rate (TSR):** Target is > 95%. Drops below 90% trigger automated alerts.
- **Customer Dispute Ratio:** Target is < 0.1% of transactions.
- **KYC Resubmission Compliance:** Immediate action on expiring documents.
- *Review:* Silver and Gold tier merchants with declining TSR will receive a support call from their regional operational manager to troubleshoot hardware or local internet connection issues.

## 5. Partner Suspension & Reactivation Policy
- **Suspension Triggers:**
  - Fraudulent activity (e.g., cash out using fake Aadhaar biometric, split transactions to cheat AePS commission).
  - Continuous low compliance (not completing Re-KYC within 30 days of notice).
  - Customer chargebacks/disputes exceeding 1.5% of monthly transaction count.
- **Reactivation Process:**
  - The partner must submit a physical verification request form signed by the regional distributor.
  - The compliance officer performs a physical site inspection (geo-tagged and time-stamped photo of the merchant inside the shop).
  - Resolution SLA: Reactivation is decided within **3 working days** post physical verification.

## 6. Grievance Redressal for Partners
If a partner is unsatisfied with Eko Support decisions:
- **Level 1:** File ticket through Eko Partner Portal (SLA 24 hours).
- **Level 2 (Grievance Officer):** If ticket not resolved within 5 days, escalate to `grievance@eko.co.in` (SLA 48 hours).
- **Level 3 (Nodal Officer):** Compliance escalation to `nodalofficer@eko.co.in` (SLA 7 working days).
